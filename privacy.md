## Privacy

This site uses anonymous analytics to understand aggregate visits, reading time, scroll depth, and link clicks.

### What is collected

- The page path that was opened
- Approximate active time on the page
- Approximate maximum scroll depth
- Link click totals
- The date used for daily aggregate totals

### What is not collected

- Names, email addresses, or account details
- Analytics cookies or `localStorage`
- A persistent visitor identifier
- Cross-site tracking or advertising profiles
- Session replay or keystroke recording

This setup is designed to measure visits and clicks in aggregate, not to identify who someone is.

### Browser controls

If your browser sends `Global Privacy Control` or `Do Not Track`, the analytics script does not run.

### Site preferences

The site stores your theme preference in your browser so the selected light or dark mode can be remembered on your device.
