## Privacy

This site uses anonymous analytics for aggregate site statistics and improvement.

- Measures page views, active time, scroll depth, exit pages, and link clicks.
- Does not use analytics cookies, visitor IDs, cross-site tracking, ad profiling, or session replay.
- Respects `Global Privacy Control` and `Do Not Track`.
- Stores your theme preference on your device for light and dark mode.
